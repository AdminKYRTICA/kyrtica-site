FAQs

Is Kyrtica a marketplace or a software?

Both: we connect retailers with verified wholesalers and provide tools to manage sourcing, logistics, and integrations.

Do you ship internationally?

We’re focused on India-first logistics. Cross-border is planned for a later phase.

How do payments work?

Retailers pay wholesalers directly by invoice; Kyrtica facilitates workflow and communication.
