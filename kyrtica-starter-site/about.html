About Kyrtica

Kyrtica is building a unified B2B platform that connects wholesalers and retailers. We focus on transparent pricing, logistics, and easy website integrations so you can scale without juggling tools.

What we’re solving

  • Fragmented catalogs and outdated price lists
  • Endless back-and-forth on quotes and MOQs
  • Manual dispatch planning and tracking

How it works

  1. Wholesalers list products, pricing tiers, and availability.
  2. Retailers request quotes, negotiate, and confirm orders.
  3. Logistics partners provide live rates and pickups.
